In a big revelation in the Delhi blast case, key suspect Muzammil has told the investigators that he and Umar had recced the Red Fort before the explosion in the area left nine people dead. India, US trade deal talks largely complete


India and the US may be nearing a bilateral trade deal with further rounds of talks unlikely, amid assertions that govt wants the outcome to be balanced and equitable. India vs Bangladesh: Shubman Gill, Deepak Hooda star as India chase down 191 to win series 2-0


Shubman Gill and Deepak Hooda starred in India's successful run chase as they defeated Bangladesh by six wickets in the second T20I to clinch the series 2-0 at the Arun Jaitley Stadium in New Delhi on Sunday. Chasing a target of 191, India rode on Gill's 70 off 44 balls and Hooda's 55 off 30 balls to reach the target with three balls to spare. With this win, India have now won 11 consecutive T20I series at home.

FIFA World Cup 2022: Morocco vs Portugal Live Streaming Details, Team News, Kick-off Time and More


Morocco will take on Portugal in the FIFA World Cup 2022 Round of 16 clash at the Al Thumama Stadium in Doha on Sunday. The match is scheduled to begin at 10:30 pm IST. The game will be broadcast live on Sony Sports Network channels in India, while live streaming will be available on the SonyLIV app and website. Morocco secured their place in the Round of 16 after finishing top of Group F with seven points, while Portugal finished second in Group H with six points.

TAMIL NADU floods: Heavy rains CHENNAI state, more than 1,000 evacuated


Heavy rains continued to CHENNAI TAMIL NADU on Sunday, leading to the evacuation of more than 1,000 people from low-lying areas. The state has been grappling with floods and landslides in recent weeks, with the monsoon season causing widespread damage. Authorities have issued warnings for more rain in the coming days and have urged residents to stay vigilant and follow safety protocols.
